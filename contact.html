FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

What shipping methods do you offer?

We offer standard shipping (5-8 business days, free on orders over ₹50), expedited shipping (2-3 business days, ₹9.99), and express shipping (1-2 business days, ₹14.99). All orders are carefully packaged to ensure your gifts arrive in perfect condition.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unused items in original packaging. Personalized and custom items are non-returnable unless defective. To initiate a return, contact our support team and we'll provide a prepaid shipping label.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and gift cards. All transactions are encrypted and securely processed. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.

Can I include a personalized message with my gift?

Absolutely! During checkout, you'll have the option to add a personalized message. We'll include a beautifully designed gift card with your message inside the package. You can also choose from our selection of premium gift wrapping options.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number on our website or the carrier's site to track your package in real time. You can also check your order status in your account dashboard.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by destination. Customs duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the recipient. We recommend checking our international shipping policy for details.

Can I cancel or modify my order?

You can cancel or modify your order within 1 hour of placing it. After that, the order enters our processing system and may no longer be editable. Contact our support team immediately if you need to make changes, and we'll do our best to help.

How do coupon codes work?

Simply copy the coupon code from our deals page and enter it at checkout. The discount will be applied automatically. Each code has specific terms and expiration dates, so be sure to check the details. Codes cannot be combined with other offers unless stated.
